Best Haircut for Looksmaxxing: Maximize Your Mog Score

Why Hair Affects Your Score

Omoggle's AI scans the full visible face area — hair framing directly influences how the algorithm reads facial proportions and feature prominence.

General Principles

Expose the forehead

Hair over the forehead blocks symmetry measurement. Clean forehead exposure consistently correlates with higher scores.

High contrast cut (volume on top, tight sides)

The undercut or fade creates a visual illusion of a longer, narrower face, emphasizing jawline definition and cheekbone prominence.

Pull hair back for Omoggle sessions

Any hair over the face creates measurement interference. Tie it back before playing.
