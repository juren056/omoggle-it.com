Looksmaxxing for Women: What the AI Actually Measures

Does Omoggle Score Women Differently?

Omoggle's AI uses the same six facial metrics for all users — facial symmetry, canthal tilt, jawline definition, cheekbone prominence, skin clarity, and overall harmony. However, how those metrics are weighted in the context of female faces appears to differ slightly based on community observations. Female faces tend to score higher on harmony and skin clarity metrics, while jawline sharpness is weighted less aggressively than for male faces.

This is consistent with PSL Scale theory, which acknowledges different beauty standards for male and female faces while still measuring the same underlying structural features.

Which Metrics Matter Most for Women on Omoggle?

1. Facial symmetry

The same as male scoring — facial symmetry is the top-weighted metric. Even lighting, centered camera position, and neutral expression give you the cleanest symmetry reading.

2. Canthal tilt

Positive canthal tilt (outer eye corner higher than inner) is consistently associated with higher scores for both male and female faces. For women, this metric is often enhanced by correct eye makeup — a subtle upward wing at the outer corner visually mimics positive tilt.

3. Skin clarity

Community data suggests skin clarity is weighted more heavily in female face scoring. Clear, even-toned skin with minimal texture or redness scores significantly higher. This makes our skincare routine guide especially relevant for female Omoggle users.

4. Cheekbone prominence

High, defined cheekbones score well for female faces on the PSL scale. Contouring makeup and body fat reduction both enhance cheekbone visibility.

Looksmaxxing Techniques for Women

Skincare first

The ROI on skincare is higher for women on Omoggle than almost any other intervention. A consistent routine (cleanser, niacinamide, SPF) can raise your skin clarity score noticeably within 4–6 weeks. See the full looksmaxxer skincare guide.

Haircut and framing

Hair framing affects how Omoggle's AI perceives your face shape. Hair that pulls back or frames the face to emphasize cheekbones and eye area scores better than hair that covers the face or creates visual clutter around the jawline. Read more in our haircut guide.

Body composition

Reducing facial fat percentage reveals cheekbones and improves facial harmony scores. Even modest fat loss (5–8 lbs) can visibly sharpen a female face's cheekbone-to-jaw ratio.

Camera optimization

The same rules apply: camera slightly above eye level, natural side lighting, neutral background. These are covered in detail in our Omoggle winning guide.
