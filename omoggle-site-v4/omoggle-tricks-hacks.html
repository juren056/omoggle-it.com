10 Omoggle Tricks to Boost Your Score Fast

Can You Game the Omoggle AI?

Yes. Omoggle's AI measures biometric markers from a webcam image. Every factor that changes that image changes your score.

Camera Tricks

1. The overhead angle trick

Camera 20–30cm above eye level. Consistently produces a 0.3–0.7 point increase. The #1 trick used by top players.

2. Slight face tilt

5–10 degree tilt toward your better side. Find it with our AI Analyzer.

3. 60–70% face fill

Zoom out slightly to reduce wide-angle lens distortion.

Lighting Tricks

4. One-sided window light

Natural light from a single window at 45° adds dimension. Free.

5. Reflector card

White cardboard opposite your window fills shadows on the dark side of your face.

Appearance Tricks

6. Play right after washing your face

Clean, fresh skin scores better on clarity metrics.

7. Pull hair completely back

Removes interference from facial landmark detection.

8. Subtle jaw clench

Very slight masseter engagement adds 10–15% more visible jawline definition.

Session Tricks

9. Play in the morning

0.3–0.5 point difference versus evening. See: sleep guide.

10. Calibrate with PSL Scanner first

Adjust setup until solo scan reaches your target, then compete.
