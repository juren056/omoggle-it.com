Omoggle FAQ: 20 Most Common Questions Answered

General Questions

What is Omoggle?

Omoggle is a webcam platform that pairs two random strangers and uses AI to score both faces on the PSL Scale (0–10). Higher scorer wins and is declared the "Mogger." Both players gain or lose ELO points. Full guide: What is Omoggle?

Is Omoggle free?

Basic access is free. Omoggle Pro ($10/month) unlocks analytics and priority matching. Solo PSL Scanner is also free.

Is Omoggle the same as Omegle?

No. Omegle was a general random chat service that shut down in 2023. Omoggle is a purpose-built face-rating competition platform. Full comparison: Omoggle vs Omegle.

Is Omoggle allowed on Twitch?

Yes. As of May 5th, 2026, Twitch updated its Community Guidelines to allow Omoggle. Full story: Twitch rule change.

Scoring Questions

What does Omoggle's AI measure?

Six metrics: facial symmetry, canthal tilt, jawline definition, cheekbone prominence, skin clarity, and overall harmony. Full breakdown: facial features guide.

What is a good Omoggle score?

Most real faces score 5.0–7.0. A 6.0+ puts you in HTN (top 10–15%). 7.0+ is Chadlite — genuinely rare. No confirmed Chad or Slayer player exists as of May 2026. See: PSL scale guide.

Does lighting affect your score?

Yes, significantly. Natural side lighting is optimal. Overhead lighting, backlight, and screen glow all lower scores. Full guide: camera setup.

Can you improve your Omoggle score?

Yes — setup optimization (angle, lighting, distance) can raise scores by 0.5–2.0 points immediately. Looksmaxxing produces slower but more permanent improvements. See: how to win Omoggle.

Looksmaxxing Questions

What is looksmaxxing?

Systematically improving your appearance through skincare, gym training, posture, and techniques like mewing. See: looksmaxxing 101.

Does mewing work?

Evidence supports it in adolescents. In adults, changes are modest. Most people report modest jaw definition improvement over 1–3 years. Honest review: mewing guide.

How long does looksmaxxing take?

Skincare: 4–6 weeks. Jawline from fat loss: 8–16 weeks. Significant tier improvement: 6–12 months of consistent effort.
