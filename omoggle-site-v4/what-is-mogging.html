What Does Mogging Mean? The Complete Definition

The Definition of Mogging

To mog someone means to physically dominate or outclass them in appearance. The winner is the Mogger; the loser has been Mogged.

Where Does It Come From?

The term derives from AMOG (alpha male of group), shortened and evolved into a verb. It originated in bodybuilding communities and was adopted by looksmaxxing culture to describe facial dominance.

Mogging on Omoggle

On Omoggle, mogging is formalized: the AI scans both faces and declares the higher-scorer the Mogger. The "MOGGED" screen became a viral meme after Twitch streams went public.

Types of Mogging

Height mogging, frame mogging, and looksmogging (face mogging) are the main categories. Omoggle measures face mogging directly using the PSL Scale.
