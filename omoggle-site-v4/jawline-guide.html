How to Improve Your Jawline for a Higher Mog Score

Why Jawline Matters on Omoggle

Jawline definition is one of the six primary metrics measured by Omoggle. It's also the metric most responsive to lifestyle changes — making it the highest-ROI target for looksmaxxing.

3 Proven Approaches

1. Reduce body fat

The most impactful change. Facial fat in the lower face is the primary cause of an undefined jawline. Aim for 12–15% body fat for optimal facial definition.

2. Compound gym training

Heavy compound lifts stimulate testosterone and growth hormone. Long-term, this contributes to facial masculinization including jaw development. See: gym face guide.

3. Mewing

Consistent tongue posture applies upward pressure on the palate. Long-term practitioners report improved jaw definition.
