Asmongold's Omoggle Run: 3 Wins, 4 Losses Explained

Asmongold Tries Omoggle

Asmongold — the self-described "roach king" of Twitch — jumped on the Omoggle trend and had a surprisingly competitive run. He won his first several matches, declaring: "If I can mog like a 19-year-old at 35, I feel like that's pretty impressive."

The Female Opponent Loss

His run was halted when he faced a female opponent who beat him 6.4 to 6.2. The narrow margin surprised viewers expecting a lopsided result. Asmongold's response: "This game's broken." The clip circulated widely as another example of Omoggle's unpredictability.

What does the score tell us?
