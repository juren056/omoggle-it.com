Omoggle Platform Updates — May 2026

PSL Scanner Mode: Use Omoggle Without Competing

One of the most requested features since Omoggle's launch — a solo scan mode — has been confirmed as available. The PSL Scanner allows you to analyze your own face using the same AI system as competitive matches, without being paired with an opponent. This is ideal for testing your camera setup and understanding which metrics you score highest on.

Recent Platform Updates

ELO system refinement

Omoggle tweaked its ELO calculation in late April to reduce point swings caused by lighting outliers and camera trolling (opponents holding objects in front of their face). The update was designed to make the leaderboard more reflective of actual facial scores.

Omoggle Pro subscription

Omoggle LLC launched an optional Pro tier at $10/month, offering priority matching, detailed post-match analytics, and historical score tracking. The free tier remains fully functional for standard matches.

What's Coming

Based on community speculation and hints from the Omoggle TikTok account, upcoming features may include a mobile app, score history graphs, and a friends-list battle mode. No official release dates have been confirmed.
