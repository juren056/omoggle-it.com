Omoggle vs Omegle: Full Comparison (2026)

Quick Summary

Omegle was a random text and video chat site with no purpose — shut down November 2023 due to safety issues. Omoggle is a purpose-built competitive face-rating platform using PSL Scale AI. Different products that share a naming style.

Why Did Omegle Shut Down?

Founder Leif K-Brooks cited unsustainable legal costs related to child safety failures. Its closure left a gap that Omoggle partially fills — though with a completely different use case.
