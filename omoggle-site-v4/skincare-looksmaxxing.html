The Looksmaxxer's Skincare Routine (Under $40)

Why Skincare Raises Your Omoggle Score

Skin clarity is directly measured by Omoggle's AI. It's the most rapidly improvable of the six metrics — results show in weeks.

The 3-Step Core Routine

Step 1: Gentle cleanser (morning + night)

CeraVe Hydrating Cleanser (~$15). Fragrance-free, won't strip natural oils or cause rebound redness.

Step 2: Niacinamide serum (morning)

The Ordinary Niacinamide 10% + Zinc (~$8). Reduces pores, evens skin tone, minimizes redness.

Step 3: SPF 50 sunscreen (morning)

EltaMD UV Clear (~$40). UV damage is the #1 cause of uneven skin tone. Daily SPF prevents new damage.
