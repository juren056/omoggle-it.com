Omoggle Tier List: All 8 Ranks Decoded

The 8 Official Tiers

Molecule (0–500 ELO)

Starting tier. Often assigned to poor lighting or non-face images. Don't take a Molecule result seriously — fix your camera setup first.

Sub3 (501–1,000 ELO)

Below average PSL. Improvable with looksmaxxing and setup optimization.

LTN — Low Tier Normie (1,001–1,500 ELO)

Below average. Weak jawline or asymmetry. Highly improvable with looksmaxxing.

MTN — Mid Tier Normie (1,501–2,000 ELO)

The most common tier. Average in all measured metrics. The median Omoggle player.

HTN — High Tier Normie (2,001–3,000 ELO)

Clearly above average. Top 10–15% of the player base.

Chadlite (3,001–3,500 ELO)

Currently the highest occupied tier. The #1 player sits at ~3,210 ELO.

Chad (3,501–4,000 ELO)

Vacant as of May 2026. Requires near-perfect scores across all six metrics.

Slayer (4,001+ ELO)

Theoretical. Essentially unachievable — represents a 9.0+ PSL face.
