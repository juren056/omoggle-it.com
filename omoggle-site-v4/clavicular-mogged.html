Clavicular Gets Mogged and Ragequits — The Clip That Went Viral

What Happened?

On April 29th, 2026, Braden "Clavicular" — the most prominent looksmaxxing content creator on Twitch — was playing Omoggle live on stream. Known for his content on looksmaxxing and his naturally high PSL score, Clavicular was expected to dominate on the platform. Instead, he lost to an opponent and proceeded to end his stream, saying on camera: "I'm literally ending if I lose."

The Viral Clip

X user @ImperiumFirst posted a clip of the moment captioned "Clavicular ragequit his stream after getting mogged by an Indian on Omoggle." The post accumulated over 68,000 likes within a week — becoming one of the most-shared Omoggle clips of May 2026. The clip spread to TikTok, Reddit, and YouTube shorts, significantly amplifying Omoggle's viral reach.

Why did it resonate?

The irony was the story: the face of the looksmaxxing movement, a man who built an audience by scoring himself highly on the PSL Scale, was humbled by an anonymous opponent on the same platform he'd championed. The meme wrote itself.

Clavicular's Actual Omoggle Score

Despite the viral loss, Clavicular's overall Omoggle record is competitive. Community tracking puts his win rate above 60%, and he sits in the upper HTN to low Chadlite range on the tier list — which is genuinely strong. The viral clip was an outlier, not representative of his overall performance.
