Best Omegle Alternative 2026: Omoggle and Random Chat Sites

Updated May 31, 2026 · 7 min read

Omegle shut down in November 2023. Since then millions of users have been searching for a replacement. If you want random video chat that also tells you something about yourself — not just who you will meet — Omoggle is the most interesting option in 2026.

What Happened to Omegle?

The Shutdown

Omegle's founder Leif K-Brooks closed the site permanently in November 2023 after 14 years, citing the immense cost of fighting platform misuse. At its peak Omegle had tens of millions of monthly users. None of the current alternatives are run by the same team.

Other Omegle Alternatives in 2026

Standard Random Video Chat Sites

If you want the original Omegle experience without competitive scoring:

  • OmeTV — Largest user base. Country and gender filters. Active 24/7.
  • Emerald Chat — Better moderation. Interest matching. Smaller but safer.
  • Chatroulette — Original Omegle competitor, still active.
  • CamSurf — Mobile-friendly. No account required.
  • Monkey — Built as Omegle successor. Includes duo mode.

Which Should You Use?

Matching Platform to Your Goal
