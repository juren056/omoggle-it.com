Facial Features Guide: All 6 Metrics Omoggle Measures

The 6 Metrics Omoggle Measures

1. Facial Symmetry (~22% weight)

The highest-weighted metric. How closely left and right face halves mirror each other. Improve: correct posture, center camera, sleep on back. See: symmetry guide.

2. Canthal Tilt (~18% weight)

Angle of eye corners — positive (outer higher) scores better. Improve: camera above eye level. See: canthal tilt guide.

3. Jawline Definition (~18% weight)

Sharpness of the mandible boundary. Improve: body fat reduction, gym training, mewing. See: jawline guide.

4. Cheekbone Prominence (~16% weight)

Height and definition of zygomatic bones. Improve: fat loss, camera from above. High cheekbones create a triangular facial structure that scores well.

5. Skin Clarity (~14% weight)

Evenness, texture, redness. Most rapidly improvable metric. Improve: cleanser, niacinamide, SPF. See: skincare guide.

6. Overall Harmony (~12% weight)

Composite of facial proportions — golden ratio, nose-to-face ratio, lip fullness. Largely structural, but improved indirectly by improving all other metrics.
