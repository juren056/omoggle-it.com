Gym Face: How Working Out Raises Your Mog Score

What is Gym Face?

"Gym face" is the facial transformation from consistent gym training and reduced body fat. As fat drops, the underlying bone structure — jawline and cheekbones — is revealed. Both are directly measured by Omoggle's AI.

How Much Can It Raise Your Score?

Community tracking shows 0.4–0.8 point improvement for people who drop 10–15 lbs of body fat while maintaining muscle. Main improvements: jawline definition and cheekbone prominence.

Most Effective Approaches

Caloric deficit for fat loss

400–500 calories below maintenance. Steady 0.5–1 lb/week loss while preserving muscle.

Compound lifts

Squats, deadlifts, bench press, rows. Elevated anabolic hormones contribute to facial masculinization over time.

Timeline

Visible facial changes in 8–16 weeks. Significant tier improvement takes 6–12 months.
