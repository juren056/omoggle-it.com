Looksmaxxing Before and After: Realistic Results Timeline

Updated June 1, 2026 · 8 min read

Looksmaxxing before and after results are the most searched content in the space for good reason — people want to know what is actually possible before investing months of effort. This guide covers realistic timelines with documented changes for each intervention.

Why Before and After Results Vary So Much

Starting Point Determines Outcome

The same intervention produces dramatically different before and after results depending on starting conditions. Someone going from 25% to 12% body fat will have a more dramatic facial transformation than someone going from 15% to 12%. Someone with poor skin clearing it with retinol and SPF will show more visible change than someone who already had decent skin.

The highest before-and-after potential exists when multiple factors are simultaneously suboptimal — body fat too high, skin quality poor, no consistent routine. Fixing all of these together produces compounding visible changes that no single intervention would.

Timeline by Intervention

Skincare Before and After (4-8 Weeks)

Skin clarity (14% of Omoggle score) responds fastest to intervention. A consistent routine with niacinamide, SPF 50, and gentle exfoliation produces visible changes in 4-6 weeks. Redness reduction and pore minimization appear first. Full texture improvement and evenness take 3 months. Before-and-after difference: 0.3-0.8 points on skin clarity metric. See: Skincare Guide.

Face Fat Loss Before and After (8-16 Weeks)

Face fat loss produces the most dramatic before-and-after of any looksmaxxing intervention. At 0.5-1 lb/week loss rate, facial changes appear at 8-12 weeks. Full transformation to 12-15% body fat takes 4-6 months for most starting points. Before-and-after difference: 0.8-1.8 points across jawline, cheekbones, and overall harmony metrics. See: Face Fat Loss Guide.

Gym Training Before and After (3-6 Months)

Compound training produces two relevant changes: body composition (indirectly affects face fat) and hormonal effects that contribute to facial masculinization over 3-6 months. The facial before-and-after from gym training is primarily through the body fat mechanism. Direct facial masculinization from testosterone is real but modest at 6 months. See: Gym Face Guide.

Mewing Before and After (6-24 Months)

Mewing before-and-after results in adults are modest compared to the other interventions. The evidence for significant bone remodeling is weak. Postural improvements — jaw resting position, reduced forward head posture — produce some visible changes at 6-12 months. The most dramatic mewing before-and-afters circulating online are from teenagers, where bone remodeling is more active. See: Mewing Guide.

What Changes Most Visibly

Ranked by Visual Impact

  1. Face fat loss — most dramatic overall transformation
  2. Skincare — most visible improvement in photo quality and skin clarity
  3. Camera and lighting setup — most immediate score improvement
  4. Posture correction — visible in full-body context
  5. Gym training — body composition changes, indirect facial effect
  6. Jaw training — subtle, long-timeline definition improvement
  7. Mewing — modest results in adults over long timeline
