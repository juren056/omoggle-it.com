Looksmaxxing & Omoggle Glossary: Every Term Defined

A–C

Canthal tilt

The angle from inner to outer eye corner. Positive tilt (outer higher) scores better on Omoggle. See: full guide.

Chad / Chadlite

PSL tiers 8.0–8.9 (Chad) and 7.0–7.9 (Chadlite). Chadlite is the highest currently occupied tier on Omoggle.

E–L

ELO

Competitive ranking system from chess used by Omoggle. See: ELO guide.

Facial symmetry

How closely left and right face halves mirror each other. Omoggle's #1 weighted metric. See: guide.

HTN / MTN / LTN

High / Mid / Low Tier Normie. The three most common Omoggle tiers. HTN = 6.0–6.9, MTN = 5.0–5.9, LTN = 4.0–4.9.

Looksmaxxing

Systematically improving appearance through lifestyle changes. See: full guide.

M–P

Mewing

Tongue posture technique claimed to improve jaw structure. See: mewing guide.

Mogger / Mogged

Winner and loser of an Omoggle match. See: mogging explained.

Molecule

Lowest Omoggle tier (0–500 ELO). Usually reflects poor camera setup, not true PSL score.

PSL Scale

Physical, Status, Looks — a 1–10 facial attractiveness rating system. Omoggle's scoring backbone. See: full guide.

S–T

Slayer

Highest Omoggle tier (4,001+ ELO, 9.0+ PSL). Theoretical — no confirmed player as of May 2026.

Sub3

PSL below 3.0. Omoggle tier 501–1,000 ELO.
