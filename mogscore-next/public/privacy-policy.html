Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Welcome to MogScore.wiki ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit omoggle-it.com and use our AI face analysis tools.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

When you use our AI Face Analyzer or 1v1 Mog Battle tool, you upload photos to our service. These photos are processed in real time by our AI system and are not stored, logged, or retained on our servers. Once the analysis is complete, the image data is discarded immediately.

Automatically collected information

Like most websites, we automatically collect certain technical information when you visit MogScore.wiki, including:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website (how you found us)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

This data is collected through Google Analytics and is used solely to understand how visitors use our site and to improve our content.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our AI face analysis tools
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior to improve content
  • Ensure the security and proper functioning of our website
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Photos and Image Data

Your privacy when using our AI tools is important to us:

  • No storage: Photos you upload are processed instantly and never saved to our servers
  • No training: Your photos are not used to train AI models
  • No sharing: Your photos are never shared with third parties beyond the AI processing API
  • Client-side processing: Images are compressed in your browser before being sent

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use the following cookies and tracking technologies:

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics. Google Analytics uses cookies to track visitor behavior. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Google AdSense (planned)

We may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics — website analytics (Google Privacy Policy applies)
  • Stripe — payment processing for Pro subscriptions (name, email, billing details, transaction records). See Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Clerk — user authentication (email, account identifiers)
  • Supabase — database for user points and subscription status
  • Anthropic Claude API / gptsapi.net — AI image analysis (processes photos you upload)
  • Vercel — website hosting and serverless functions
  • Google Fonts — typography

When you subscribe to MogScore Pro, Stripe collects payment information on our behalf. We store your subscription status and Stripe customer ID but do not store full credit card numbers.

6. Payment & Subscription Data

If you purchase a Pro subscription, we collect and store:

  • Stripe customer ID and subscription ID
  • Subscription plan, status, and billing period end date
  • Your account email (via Clerk) for billing correspondence

Payment card details are handled exclusively by Stripe and never touch our servers. See our Refund Policy and Terms of Service.

7. Data Retention

We retain analytics data (collected via Google Analytics) for up to 26 months, after which it is automatically deleted. Subscription and billing records are retained as required for tax and legal compliance (typically 7 years). We do not retain any photo or image data — all uploaded images are discarded immediately after analysis.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access information we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of analytics tracking
  • The right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in the Contact section below.

9. Children's Privacy

MogScore.wiki is intended for users aged 18 and older, consistent with Omoggle's own age requirements. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at:
