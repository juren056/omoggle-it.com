Agent00 Wore a Fake Forehead on Omoggle and Still Mogged People

Updated May 14, 2026

What Happened

AMP member Din "Agent00" took a unique approach to Omoggle in May 2026: instead of optimizing his camera setup or lighting like most streamers, he attached a prosthetic forehead to his head before entering the live mog arena.

The result? He won multiple consecutive rounds despite the obvious prosthetic — baffling his chat and demonstrating just how unstable Omoggle's computer vision scoring can be.

What This Reveals About the AI

Agent00's prosthetic forehead moment is actually the most revealing technical demonstration of how Omoggle's scoring works:

The AI scores landmarks, not the full picture

Omoggle's computer vision system maps facial landmark points — the corners of eyes, tip of nose, edges of jaw. A prosthetic forehead doesn't dramatically move the landmark points that matter most for scoring (eyes, canthal tilt, jawline).

Camera angle and lighting still dominate

Agent00's real advantage was likely his controlled streaming setup — good lighting, optimal camera height, clean background. These factors consistently outweigh facial features themselves, as our camera setup guide explains.

Opponents had worse setups

The other side of a mog battle matters as much as your side. If opponents have poor camera quality or bad lighting, even a prosthetic forehead won't cost you the win.

The Clip

The moment spread quickly across Twitch clips and X, adding to the growing library of evidence that Omoggle's AI is entertaining precisely because it's unpredictable. The same inconsistency that let xQc lose six in a row let Agent00 win while wearing a costume forehead.
