Facial Symmetry Guide: The Most Important Omoggle Metric

What is Facial Symmetry?

Facial symmetry measures how closely your left and right face halves mirror each other. Omoggle's AI weights it at roughly 22% of your total score — the highest of any single metric.

Why It Matters

Evolutionary biology links symmetry to perceived health. AI systems trained on human attractiveness ratings encode this pattern. Even minor improvements in perceived symmetry meaningfully raise your PSL score.

Can You Improve It?

Postural corrections

Habitual head tilting creates muscular imbalances that show as asymmetry. Correcting posture, sleeping on your back, and chewing evenly can reduce postural asymmetry over months.

Camera tricks

Perfectly centering your face and using straight-on lighting minimizes visual asymmetry impact. See: camera setup guide.
