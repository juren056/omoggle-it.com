Why Scores Vary So Much

Every documented streamer shows high variance between sessions. This is expected — Omoggle's computer vision system is optimized for controlled conditions and is highly sensitive to camera angle (15–20cm difference can swing scores by 1+ point), lighting direction, and distance. Streamers who figured this out (Jynxzi being the clearest example) maintained more consistent scores. Those who played with default streaming setups showed the highest variance.
